You are green. Orange is an enemy. You can move with the arrow keys, and attack with the space bar.
You can also run the following commands:
  • Potion (purple) - will temporarily either confuse you or double your strength
  • Health (red) - restores some health
  • new Orc("name") - generates a new Orc()
  • new Giant("name") - generate a new Giant
  • new Cat() - generate a new cat, which follows you around healing with love
  • new Player("name") - generate a new player if you die
  • Player/Orc.startABeef(target) - start a verbal quarrel between two characters
